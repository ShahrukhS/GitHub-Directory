Community Focused

Since Shopunion is based around cities, your messages will be seen by people in your community who have chosen to follow you.

  • Target the people who care about your business.
  • Don’t waste time posting for bots and other fake accounts on the internet.
Get Discovered

Every business in the community is listed on one page for easy discovery.

  • Expose your business to locals who want to support you.
  • Establish an accessible mobile presence.
Push Notifications

Users can choose to be alerted when you post so they never miss an update.

  • Time-sensitive or important updates are delivered immediately.
  • Information is sent to the user without them having to search for it.
Social Media Integration

Shopunion integrates with your current social media campaigns and lets you cross-post to Facebook and Twitter simultaneously.

  • Stop logging in to several places to post the same content.
  • Maintain a Twitter or Facebook presence even if you're not using them daily.
Scheduling

Our scheduling feature allows you to choose when you want your messages to be sent.

  • Schedule sales, events, and other information to be sent out in the future.
  • Users can view your scheduled information on your calendar in the app.
  • Information is pushed out at the specific times you specify.
Analytics

Track important metrics like views, clicks, shares, and likes for each post

  • Find out exactly how your followers are engaging with your messages.
  • We make sure you're knowledgeable about whether your time and money are working for you.
Contact and Directions

Your customers can visit your website, get directions, or call from the app.

  • Easily establish a mobile presence without building your own expensive mobile app.
  • Mobile accessibility means more calls, visits to your location, and most importantly, more sales.
E-Commerce (coming soon)

Become a Shopunion merchant and make sales directly to your customers on the app.

  • No need to set up a complicated website online.
  • Push a buy or donate button straight to your followers.

Case Study

"Shopunion is the most innovative small business marketing tool I have ever seen!" CEO, DoubleDave’s Pizzaworks

DoubleDave’s serves pizza in Austin, Texas. They signed up three locations on Shopunion and pushed out an internet special: Use coupon code "Shopunion" on your order and get $7 off.

This one push generated 60 orders across the three locations for an average order size of $28.

That's $1680 in revenue on their first push.
